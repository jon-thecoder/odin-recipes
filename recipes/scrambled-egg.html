Scrambled-eggs

Description

It is a common snack in India where people have it with the evening coffee.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Chilli and onion
  • Oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Pour a hand spoon of Oil in the fry pan with onions,chilli and salt
  2. Break the eggs and mix it in the pan until it is dry enough
  3. Serve it with Indian style coffee.
